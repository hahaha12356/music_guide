Music Terms Glossary

Click on a term to see its definition.

  • Adagio

    A tempo marking indicating that music should be played slowly. From the Italian for "at ease."

  • Chord

    A combination of three or more musical notes played simultaneously. Chords are the foundation of harmony in Western music.

  • DAW (Digital Audio Workstation)

    Software used for recording, editing, and producing audio files. Examples include Ableton Live, FL Studio, and Logic Pro.

  • Reverb

    An audio effect that creates a sense of space by simulating the reflections of sound in a room. It makes audio sound less "dry."
