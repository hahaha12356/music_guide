General Royalty-Free Music

Discover platforms offering free music for commercial use, CC licensed tracks, and royalty-free downloads for your creative projects.

About Royalty-Free Music

Royalty-free music allows creators to use audio tracks in their projects without paying ongoing royalties for each use. These platforms offer a range of licensing options, from completely free with attribution to subscription-based models. To better understand these terms, check out our guide to music licensing.

The resources in this category provide access to thousands of high-quality tracks that can be legally used in your videos, podcasts, games, and other creative works.

Featured Resources

Free Music Archive (FMA)

royalty-free music download CC licensed music free music for commercial use

Discover a vast library of high-quality, royalty-free music at Free Music Archive (FMA). Perfect for content creators, this platform offers a wide range of CC licensed music and free music downloads suitable for commercial use.

How to Use:

Simply visit the Free Music Archive website, browse through genres or search by keywords like "royalty-free music" or "free music for commercial use". Download your chosen tracks and ensure you adhere to the specific Creative Commons license for each piece.

Tips for Using Royalty-Free Music

Always Check the License

Even when music is labeled as "royalty-free," there may be specific terms and conditions. Some require attribution, while others may have restrictions on commercial use.

Provide Proper Attribution

When required, make sure to credit the artist and source properly. This is typically done in video descriptions, end credits, or in the footer of your website.

Keep Records

Save documentation of the license terms for each track you use. This can be helpful if questions arise about your right to use the music in the future.
