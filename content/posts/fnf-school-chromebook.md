## FAQ Section

**Q: Will playing FNF mods slow down my school Chromebook?**
**A:** It can. Some high-quality mods (like *Vs. Impostor V4*) have heavy assets. If you experience lag, right-click inside the game and lower the quality to "Low," or try to close other Chrome tabs to free up RAM.

**Q: Can I get in trouble for playing unblocked games?**
**A:** Generally, you won't get suspended for playing games during free time, but teachers can monitor your screen. Always prioritize your schoolwork first! If you try to install software (.exe files), that’s when IT gets mad—stick to browser versions.

**Q: Why does the screen go black when I load a mod?**
**A:** This is usually a WebGL issue. Your school Chromebook might have "Hardware Acceleration" disabled to save battery. Go to your Chrome Settings > System and ensure "Use graphics acceleration when available" is turned ON.

## Troubleshooting Tips

遇到网页模组黑屏或无声：
- 打开 Chrome 设置 > 系统，启用硬件加速；地址栏输入 `chrome://flags`，将 “GPU rasterization” 与 “Zero-copy rasterizer” 设为 Enabled。
- 清理站点数据与缓存，尤其是 KBH/Snokido 这类站点；长期缓存会让旧脚本与新资源打架。
- 关闭扩展（广告拦截、翻译、截图工具），它们会钩子注入页面导致性能下降或 WebGL 初始化失败。
- 课堂环境下不要开十几个标签页；Chromebook 的内存很紧，后台标签会把前台的帧率吃掉。
